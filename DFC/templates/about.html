DOON FIGHT CLUB

Doon Fight Club is an MMA gym located in the beautiful valley of Dehradun.Here we train in different forms of fighting style to become a complete fighter.

BOXING

Boxing is a combat sport in which two people, usually wearing protective gloves and other protective equipment such as hand wraps and mouthguards, throw punches at each other for a predetermined amount of time in a boxing ring. Being good in Boxing will elavate your striking game in MMA. It is a major part of MMA as it helps fighters to fight long range fights. Fighters like Conor McGregor and Francis Ngannou with Boxing background has gone on to become world champions in MMA . .

KICKBOXING

Kickboxing is a standup combat sport in which punches and kicks are used.It adds kick to your arsenal which is very important.There are lot of MMA greats like Mirkon Filipovic,Alistair Overeem,Stephen Thompson and Israel Adesanya who had KickBoxing Background.

BRAZILIAN JUI-JITSU

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is a martial art based on grappling and submission holds. It focuses on the skill of taking an opponent to the ground, controlling one's opponent, gaining a dominant position and using a number of techniques to force them into submission via joint locks or chokeholds. A good BJJ fighter will always have an advantage in the ground game. With skills learned from BJJ you can defeat opponents stronger than you. There are countless fighters with BJJ background who went on to become champions in MMA. Some of the greats are Royce Gracie,Demian Maia,Nate Diaz,Anderson Silva and Ryan Hall.

WRESTLING

Wrestling is a combat sport involving grappling-type techniques such as clinch fighting, throws and takedowns, joint locks, pins and other grappling holds.Many consider wrestling as the best base for MMA, as it dictates where the fight goes. Khabib Nurmagomedov,Daniel Cormier,Jon Jones,Henry Cejudo, Brock Lesner and many more greats have a wrestling background.

MUAYTHAI

Muay Thai sometimes referred to as "Thai boxing", is a martial art and combat sport that uses stand-up striking along with various clinching techniques. This discipline is known as the "art of eight limbs" as it is characterized by the combined use of fists, elbows, knees and shins.The Muay Thai striking power, its training methods and its clinching tactics are solid reasons to make muay thai part of your training. Jose Aldo,Joanna Jedrzejczyk and Jan Blachowicz are some of the greats in MMA with Muay Thai background.

MIXED MARTIAL ARTS

Mixed martial arts (MMA) is a hybrid combat sport incorporating techniques from above mentioned disciplines and many other like Karate,Taekwondo,Judo,etc.
